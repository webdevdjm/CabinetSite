Services

Services include kitchen remodeling, bath remodeling, countertops, kitchen cabinet installation, entertainment centers, custom cabinets, and much, much more! Custom Cabinet Design is a fully licensed and insured general contractor, specializing in residential cabinet installation in MA and RI.
